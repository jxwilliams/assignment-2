Travelogue: Paris

Overview

Paris is easy to enjoy on a short trip. Many sights are close together, the metro is simple to use, and there are parks and cafés everywhere.

Must-See Spots

  • Eiffel Tower
  • Louvre Museum
  • Sacré-Cœur
  • Pont Alexandre III
  • Le Marais

    • Visitor Tips

  • Buy a metro ticket pack.
  • Eat a few streets away from major sights.
  • Reserve dinner times early.
  • Wear comfortable walking shoes.

    • Main Attraction

    Eiffel Tower

    What People Say

    Paris feels calm and fun. So relaxing!
